About Our Services

At ADISUPPORT, we specialize in providing tailored sports and outdoor activity programs for children with both physical and mental disabilities. Our mission is to help each child discover their potential, build confidence, and develop essential life skills through engaging physical activities.

We understand the unique challenges that parents of children with disabilities face. That's why our programs are designed not just to benefit the children but also to provide much-needed support and respite for parents. When your child is in our care, you can have peace of mind knowing they're in a safe, nurturing environment.

Our team consists of experienced professionals who are trained in disability support and have backgrounds in sports coaching, occupational therapy, and child development. We create individualized activity plans that consider each child's abilities, interests, and goals.

Through our carefully designed programs, children learn teamwork, improve motor skills, enhance social interaction, and most importantly, have fun! We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to experience the joy of physical activity and the great outdoors.
