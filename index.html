Our Adaptive Sports Programs

We offer a diverse range of activities tailored to your abilities and interests, with expert support every step of the way.

Wheelchair Sports

Basketball, tennis, and rugby designed for wheelchair users with specialized equipment and coaching.

Adventure Outings

Accessible hiking trails, camping experiences, and nature exploration in beautiful locations.

Aquatic Activities

Swimming, hydrotherapy, kayaking, and adaptive surfing with specialized support.

Social Sports

Team-based activities that build connections while having fun in a supportive environment.

Fitness Training

Personalized strength and conditioning programs to improve overall health and performance.

Competitive Pathways

Opportunities to compete at local, state, and national levels for those seeking challenges.
