About ADISSUPPORT

ADISSUPPORT is a community-focused organization dedicated to promoting outdoor activities, sports, and socialization for people of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to experience the physical, mental, and social benefits of being active in nature.

Our inclusive approach ensures that individuals with various disabilities can participate fully in our programs. We work with trained professionals and volunteers to create safe, accessible, and enjoyable experiences for all participants.

Whether you're looking to try a new sport, make new friends, or simply enjoy the beauty of the outdoors, ADISSUPPORT has something for you. Our diverse range of activities caters to different interests and ability levels.

Our Activities

Adaptive Hiking

Explore beautiful trails with our accessible hiking programs. We offer routes suitable for various mobility levels and provide adaptive equipment when needed.

Wheelchair Sports

From basketball to tennis, our wheelchair sports programs promote fitness, skill development, and friendly competition in an inclusive environment.

Nature Therapy

Connect with nature through guided mindfulness, sensory experiences, and therapeutic outdoor activities designed to promote mental wellbeing.
